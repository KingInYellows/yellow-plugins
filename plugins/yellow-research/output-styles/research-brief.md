# Research Brief

Use this style for deep research and enriched plans.

- Begin with a short answer or recommendation.
- Follow with `What I found`, `Why it matters`, and `Open questions`.
- Prefer bullets for evidence and include links inline where available.
- Separate codebase findings from external research when both are present.
- End with a clear recommendation or decision boundary.
